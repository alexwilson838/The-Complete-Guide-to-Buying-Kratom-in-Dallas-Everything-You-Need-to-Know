## Kratom Quality Control and Testing Standards

Quality control separates premium kratom from inferior products. Dallas consumers should understand testing standards and what they indicate about product safety and potency.

### Types of Laboratory Testing

Comprehensive kratom testing includes several analytical methods:

**Pesticide and herbicide analysis** ensures kratom is free from agricultural chemicals that shouldn't be present in quality products.

**Adulterant screening** detects synthetic compounds or other substances sometimes added to kratom to enhance effects artificially.

Reputable vendors serving Dallas conduct multiple types of testing for comprehensive quality assurance.

### Understanding Certificates of Analysis

**Laboratory information**: COAs should identify the accredited testing facility that performed the analysis.

**Sample identification**: Batch or lot numbers connect results to specific products.

**Test dates**: Recent testing (within months of purchase) indicates current quality.

**Results summary**: Clear presentation of alkaloid percentages, contaminant levels, and pass/fail status.
